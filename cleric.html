Overview

The cleric is a strong support class and the only true healer currently in the game. This guide will be for two very similar builds that will focus on the best of both worlds for single target and AoE heals. The first build is more mana friendly while the later build has more skillful gameplay that can result in strong instant cast combos for easy life saving moments, or strong moments of burst and crowd control.

Essence Infusion Talents

While active, your spells consume Divine Power instead of mana or health.
Judgment         18s Cooldown
30m Range         4.2s Cast

If cast on an ally, restore health equal to 1500% healing power.
If cast on an enemy deal 280% magic power radiant damage and apply burning. If the target is Shaken, the caster is healed for the damage dealt.
Resplendent Beam     20s Cooldown
30m Range           1.4s cast

Fire a beam of healing energy towards target ally. Charging this spell allows the beam to bounce up to 4 additional friendly targets closest to the original target. The initial hit heals for 300% healing power and reduces with each bounce.

4m Range           Instant Cast

Heal target in melee range for 500% healing.
Plentiful Mend

Mend has two additional charges.
Escalating Mend

Mend's healing is increased each time it is used in quick succession.

30m Range   3 Charges   4.2s Cast

Instantly heal ally target for 250% healing. This may be used during other abilities.
Deliverance
30m Range         3.7s Cast

Charge up healing energy that heals a target ally upon release, scaling in power the longer it is charged, up to 1000% healing.

30m Range   2 Charges   Instant Cast

Heal for 60% healing every seconds, lasts 8 seconds.
Abundent Soothing Glow

Soothing glow has an aditional charge.
Casting soothing glow on a target already under its effect extends its duration by its base duration.
Redemptive Deliverance

Deliverance heals more based on the missing health percentage of the target.
Bless Weapon
30m Range           1s Cast

Imbue your target's weapon with radiant light, granting them Bless Weapon. Weapon combo finishers deal an additional 5% radiant damage and apply Bless Weapon: Mana Regen
Bless Weapon: Mana Regen -Regenerate mana ever 1.5 seconds. Last 6 seconds per application up to 15 seconds.
Bountiful Bless Weapon

Whenever an ally attacks whose weapon you have blessed, you also gain Bless Weapon: Mana Regen
Righteous Bless Weapon

While Bless Weapon is active, weapon attacks have a 20% chance to proc an additional 20% radiant damage and apply burning.
Divine Retribution  

Divine Flare deals 250% radiant damage an applies Silenced to enemies within its area of effect when it detonates. Damage is divided amoungst targets hit.
Purify
30m Range           Instant Cast
Remove up to 3 dispellable debuffs from an ally.
Defiant Light         30s Cooldown
30m Range         Instant Cast
Heal target ally for 50% of their max health over 10 seconds. If they would recieve fatal damage during this time the effect is consumed and they are instead healed for 25% of their max health.
Barrier             10s Cooldown
30m Range           Instant Cast

Sacrifice 25% of your max health and apply that much temporary health to a target ally for 10 seconds. Does not stack.
Devine Flare         15s Cooldown
30m Range           Instant Cast
Place a targeted area of effect on the ground that heals all targets after several seconds. Heals for 1000% healing divided amoungst each target.
Condemn           30s Cooldown
30m Range           1.8s Cast
Apply Incapcitated for 10 seconds to enemy target. If target is Conflagurating, target becomes stunned for 4 seconds instead.
Consecrating Wave     18s Cooldown
20m Range           Instant Cast
Send forth a wave of radiance that deals 150% radiant damage and applies Volatile to all enemies in front of you.
Dispell 1 buff from each target hit that is silenced.
Restorative Consecrating Wave
Consecrating Wave additionally heals you and up to 10 allies for 250% healing.
Divine Protection
Divine Flare applies Divine Protection to allies inside of its area of effect before detonation.
Divine Protection - Magical Mitigation increased by 50% for 5 seconds.
Unyielding Wings of Salvation
Wings of Salvation breaks all disabling effects on the caster.
Wings of Salvation   30s Cooldown
30m Range           Instant Cast
Sprout phoenix-like wings and dash to the target ally, granting Wings of Salvation to the caster and target on arivial.
Wings of Salvation - Gain a shield for 250% magic power.
Communal Restoration   10s Cooldown
30m Range       2.3s Cast
Restore health equal to 150% healing to all party memebers in range and apply Communal Restoration.
Communal Restoration - apply a shield for 10% of targets max health and heal for 30% healing every second for 6 seconds.
Chains of Restraint       30s Cooldown
30m Range             2.1s Cast
Conjure a circle of radiant chains at target location for 8 seconds that deal 40% radiant damage every second and applies Snared to targets within area.
Each Weakened target hit by the initial pulse becomes rooted.
Binding Chains of Restraint
Chains of Restraint's final tick applies stunned for 3 seconds.

Gear:
While leveling you should just always prioritize whatever the strongest gear you have available to you. Try to focus on using gear with useful stats like Int/Mentality/Wisdom but anything is better than an empty slot.
For weapons you can get away with anything that has magical power on it. The recommended combo would be either a scepter with a focus or shield, or 2h spellsword for you melee slots. For ranged the best weapon skills for cleric come from wand or spellbook, but you can make use of any spellbow as well.

    Abilities

    • Essence Infusion: Allows you to spend Divine Power instead of mana or health when casting abilities.
    • Divine Infusion: Allows you to spend Divine Power to instantly finish the cast of an ability.
    • Divine Power: Class resource that builds from healing or damage. While at high resource levels you chance to crit is increased.
    • Mend: Launched a slow moving bolt of low healing at target.
    • Resurrect: Brings ally back from death.
    • Smite: Deals a small amount of radiant damage, if the target is buring it upgrades the burn to Conflagurate.
    • Judgment: Can be cast on either an enemy for a moderate amount of damage or on an ally for a very large amount of healing. Causes burning on enemies.
    • Resplendent Beam: Charged abilty that will heal up to five allies based on charge time.
    • Healing Touch: Large instant cast heal that must be used at melee range.
    • Flash Cure: Instant cast spell that heals for a moderate amount. Has three charges.
    • Deliverance: Charged ability that will up to an extremely large amount based on charge time.
    • Soothing Glow: Applies a small heal over time to an ally for a moderate amount of time, has two charges.
    • Bless Weapon: Buff applied to an ally that allows for extra damage and mana regeneration.
    • Defiant Light: Applies a large heal over time to an ally and applies a cheat death affect for its duration.
    • Barrier: Sacrafice your own health to apply a shield for the same amount to an ally for a small amount of time.
    • Purify: Removes up to three debuffs from an ally per cast.
    • Divine Flare: Places a circle on the ground that errupts for healing and damage split between targets.
    • Condemn: Applies a long sleep affect on enemies, if target is Conflagurating this is instead a four second stun.
    • Consecrating Wave: Instant cast ability that causes healing and damage in a cone in front of you.
    • Communal Restoration: Applies a small heal, a small heal over time and a small shield affect to all party memebers within range.
    • Wings of Salvation: Dashes you to a target ally.
    • Chains of Restraint: Places a circle on the ground that ticks for damage over time and applies snared. This can be upgraded to a stun.

    Tips & Tricks

    • Positioning is Key: Make sure to keep youself in a safe spot towards the rear of the group. Wings is your only get out jail free card so make sure you have before trying to push to the front.
    • Cooldown Management: You have quite a few low cooldown spells. Knowing when things like Wings or Defiant Light are off cooldown is imperative to your survival.
    • Use Buffs Wisely: Unless you are told to put Bless Weapon on a tank, you should always try to put it on your highest geared mage or ranger.
    • Coordinate with Tanks: Communicate with your tank to ensure you have proper timing on cooldowns so they don't die to spike damage.
    • Keep an Eye on Mana: Use mana salves when needed and use your ranged weapon whenever you can. Refreshing from your weapon attacks goes a long way to keeping high mana.

    The Wombo Combo:

    Cleric has a decent combo they can pull off for damage when needed. Its not a ton of damage but it can be enough to turn a fight or secure a kill.
    The combo itself is the same no matter what talents you took, the difference is mainly in the timing. With Divine Infusion you will be able to instantly cast several spells in the combo. You should instant cast Judgment and Condemn if you can.
    The combo: Judgment > Smite > Condemn > Divine Flare > Chains of Restraint > Consecrating Wave
